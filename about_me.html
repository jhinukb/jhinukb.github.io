About Me

Jhinuk Barman



Hey there! First, I would like to thank you for visiting my website! So..you're probably wondering, who am I? I am Jhinuk, a senior majoring in Data Science with a concentration in Psychology at the University of California, Berkeley. I am graduating in December 2019. My journey into Data Science began when I took Data8, the introductory Data Science course. Ever since then, I have been inspired by the intersection of technology and education. I also dabble in graphic design through various positions.
How am I involved with my university? I am currently working in the Curriculum Development and Peer Advising Team for the Divison of Data Sciences. I was also part of the analytics committee for the Data Science Society where I collaborated on data science research projects. I am part of the Society of Women Engineers and AFX Dance Outreach Committee where I work on Graphic Design.
My life is not solely composed of academics: In my free time (when I have any), I enjoy urban dancing on the AFX Dance Team, baking (I took a baking DeCal!), hiking and playing the piano. I am seeking internships in Data Science, Software Engineering, Computational Biology or Graphic Design.

If you are interested, feel free to contact me at jhinuk.barman@berkeley.edu

Have a wonderful day! ~Jhinuk Barman
